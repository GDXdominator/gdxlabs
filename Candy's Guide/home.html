Who is this cat ?

What is the Forest of Doom (and Darkness)?

The Forest of Doom (and Darkness) (full name “Forest of Doom and Darkness and Evil and Gloom and Madness and Evil and” (legend says that whoever wrote the name was taken before they could finish it)) is a large forest cursed long ago by an ancient curse, becoming a perfect home to many strange or accursed creatures, deities, and entities. Those unfortunate enough to have settled in the Forest of Doom (and Darkness) had learned to adapt to their new dangerous surroundings, one group in particular being The Candled, which I am apart of.
