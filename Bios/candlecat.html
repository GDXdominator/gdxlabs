    Name: Candle Cat

    ID: They/them

    Species: Candled American Shorthair Humanoid

    Genre(s): Dark ambient + ambient, drone, drone metal

Candle Cat, nicknamed “Candy,” is a shy and reserved cat living in the Forest of Doom (and Darkness) alongside the rest of the Candle Trio. Had a spiritual experience at a sunn O))) concert one day and quickly became infatuated with drone metal. Despite being the lab's quietest member, Candle Cat produces the lab's loudest music. They like to spend their time in the lab producing sounds of the forest and soaking in amp feedback. Big advocate for ear protection.
