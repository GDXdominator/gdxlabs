    Name: Candle Goat

    ID: He/it

    Species: Candled Alpine Goat Humanoid

    Genre(s): Black metal + ambient black metal, doom metal, cutting penis black metal

Candle Goat, full name Billy Bathory Goat (which he is very proud of), is a reclusive, metalhead goat living in the Forest of Doom (and Darkness) alongside the rest of the Candle Trio. Listened to Darkthrone's “A Blaze in the Northern Sky” one day and was never the same since. Being the only member to have never upgraded their recording gear from what can only be described as a toaster, Candle Goat has devoted his music to only the rawest form truest to his heart and emotions, whether that be his struggle with depression, the beauties of the forest, or pure mayhem and evil. Is a fan of nu metal and doesn't want to admit it.
